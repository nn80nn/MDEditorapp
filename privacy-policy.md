# Privacy Policy for MDEditor

**Last updated: April 27, 2026**

---

## 1. Overview

MDEditor ("the App") is a Markdown editor for Android developed as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

**Account data**
When you register, we store your email address and a securely hashed password (bcrypt). We never store your password in plain text.

**Authentication token**
After login, a JWT token is saved locally on your device to keep you signed in. It is not shared with third parties.

**Documents**
Markdown files you choose to upload are stored on our server and associated with your account. Files you keep locally never leave your device.

**No analytics, no tracking**
We do not collect usage statistics, device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any behavioral data.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- To authenticate you and provide access to your documents
- To store and serve your uploaded files
- We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties

---

## 4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on a private server. Documents are stored only as long as your account exists. We do not use third-party cloud providers (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.).

---

## 5. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| INTERNET | Connecting to the server for sync and auth |
| READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android ≤ 12) | Importing local .md files |
